Our story

A hillside greenhouse, 8 years in the making

What began in 2018 as a single greenhouse and a passion for phalaenopsis has grown into one of the region's most trusted specialty orchid farms — today supplying retail and trade partners nationwide alongside our direct-to-home customers.

We grow in natural-light greenhouses, using properly treated well water and bark-based media that lets roots breathe as nature intended. Every plant is propagated from proven parent stock and raised for a minimum of 18 months before it leaves our care — a standard our retail and trade partners have come to rely on.

Growing tips

Caring for your phalaenopsis

☀

Light

Bright, indirect light near an east-facing window. Avoid harsh afternoon direct sun, which scorches leaves.

💧

Watering

Water thoroughly every 7–10 days. Allow roots to dry slightly between waterings. Never let the plant sit in standing water.

🌡

Temperature

Ideal range is 18–28 °C. A 5–8 °C drop at night for 4 weeks in autumn encourages new flower spikes.

🌿

Feeding

Use a balanced orchid fertiliser at quarter-strength every second watering during active growth. Reduce in winter.
